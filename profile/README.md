## Products

### [Augmentum OS](https://augmentum.computer)

Most operating systems are environments you work *in*. Augmentum OS is an environment that works *with you*.

It runs a cognitive layer in the background — watching your patterns, learning what slows you down, proposing automation. Not cloud AI phoning home. Local inference, on your hardware, under your terms. The OS asks before it learns. It tells you when it is learning. You can turn it off.

Spread across multiple machines, your fleet becomes one organism. A 3D spatial dashboard shows data flowing between nodes in real time. Your machines coordinate distributed AI inference — heavy models on powerful nodes, light models on laptops — routing automatically based on what the hardware can handle.

When something goes wrong, the OS tells you why in plain language. Updates show exactly what changes before applying them. Every action produces a receipt. Rollback is one click. Privacy is a system-wide mode, not a per-app checkbox — switch once and the entire OS reconfigures its behavior.

Built on NixOS. Your entire fleet — every machine, every service, every configuration — is described in a single source file. Reproducible like a recipe. Recoverable from any state. Every node identical to its specification, always.

Private alpha. Coming 2026.

### [Visage](https://github.com/sovren-software/visage)

Linux face authentication, done right. No cloud. No subprocess spawned on every unlock. A persistent daemon running local ONNX inference, credentials encrypted on disk, IR camera support, PAM integration — your face opens your machine the way a key opens a door.

Ships on Ubuntu 24.04 LTS today. Faster than the alternatives on matched hardware.

### [MrHaven](https://mrhaven.io)

A programmable asset vault on Base L2 with no custodian. The contract holds your funds. Math enforces the rules. No company stands in the middle, takes a cut, or requires your identity.

Designed from the ground up for humans and autonomous AI agents equally. An agent running its own budget, scheduling payments, enforcing release conditions — no KYC, no approval chain, no human in the loop. The protocol does not distinguish. As AI agents begin to operate with financial autonomy, this is the infrastructure they will need.
